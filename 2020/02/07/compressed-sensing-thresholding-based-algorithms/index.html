论文笔记 Dictionary-Sparse Recovery via Thresholding-Based Algorithms

Posted on 2020-02-07 Edited on 2020-02-08 In Computational Mathematics

[参考文献] Foucart, Simon. “Dictionary-sparse recovery via thresholding-based algorithms.” Journal of Fourier Analysis and Applications 22, no. 1 (2016): 6-19.

Introduction

Settings

  • The signal space: $\mathbb{C}^n$.
  • The coefficient space: $\mathbb{C}^N$.
  • Dictionary: ${D} \in \mathbb{C}^{n \times N}$ ($N > n$).
  • The measurement matrix: $A \in \mathbb{C}^{m \times n}$ ($m \ll n$).
  • Measurement error: $\varepsilon \in \mathbb{C}^m$, a bound $\lVert \varepsilon\rVert_2 \leq \eta$ is typically available.
  • Measurement Vector: $y = Af + \varepsilon \in \mathbb{C}^m$.

Problem

Given the dictionary $D$, assume that a signal $f$ is produced by $f = Dx$ where $x \in \mathbb{C}^N$ is an unknown coefficient. Let $A$ be a measurement matrix. Then the problem is to recover the signal by the observed measurement vector $y = Af + \varepsilon$.
